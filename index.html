Hi, I'm Sahitya.

I like applying math, computer science, and statistics to a (very) wide spectrum of applications, including but not limited to: clean energy, financial markets, nuclear non-proliferation, astrophysics, and genomics.

I recently graduated from Stanford University with an M.S. at the Institute for Computational and Mathematical Engineering. In My 2017, I graduated from Cornell with a B.S. in Computer Science.
